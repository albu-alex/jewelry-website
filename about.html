About us

Because functionality is our focus (there's plenty of product crash-testing footage to prove it), making the highest quality jewelry that works for you is our priority. So if something's not right, you can count on us to take care of it.

All of our jewelry products are carefully crafted at the moment of purchase in our workshop in Puerto Rico with precious metal from the locals. That means that we support the local economy and therefore, create jobs.

We know the right fit is everything when ordering a ring. That's why we are stoked to offer FREE shipping and re-turns in the US (no minimum), so shopping with us can feel as chill as a day at the beach. Just the way it should be.
